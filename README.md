# CafeCollect

Your Ultimate Cafe Rewards Companion!

## Description

CafeCollect transforms your cafe visits into rewarding experiences. Simply collect digital stickers with every purchase at participating cafes and unlock exciting rewards like free drinks, discounts, and more. No more paper cards to lose—CafeCollect keeps your progress safe and easily accessible on your phone.

### Key Features
- **Effortless Sticker Collection**: Earn stickers effortlessly by scanning a QR code at your favorite local cafes.
- **Real-Time Rewards Tracking**: Keep track of your rewards and see how close you are to your next treat.
- **Exclusive Offers**: Receive updates and special offers from your preferred coffee spots.
- **Personalized Experience**: Enjoy unique rewards from each cafe, tailored to your preferences.
